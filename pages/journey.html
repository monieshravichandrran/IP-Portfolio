My Journey

From 2001 Aug 20 to Today

August 2001

I born on Aug 20 2001 in Chennai as the only son of my parents. I spent rest of my life in Chennai

Early Life 2000-2010

Studied at Pon Vidhyasram spending my early life. Was keen in studies and cricket. Spent days as a member in School Cricket Team. AB De Villiers inspired me and pulled me to play cricket. It then became my hobby stress buster and entertainment

DAVian 2011-2019

I spent nearly 10 years at DAV and it was one among best portions of my life. With fear of new school and new friends I spent a good time there. I completed my Xth with a 10 CGPA and took Computer Science group for XIIth. I was a passioante coder and love to take CS group arose in my minds much earlier. I completed my XIIth with a 94%. The teachers I came across in DAV were a gem and their advice has always been a motivation in my life there on.

2019 SSN College of Engineering

The most excited phase of a students life is the college life. Me after completing XIIth was eager to join in college. I applied to SSN with a cutoff of 190 and got a CSE seat there. Made new friends and we used to study and play in college. The COVID was a changer in our life which forced us to stay indoors

2021 August

I attended several interns that came to the campus and couldn't make it to the list of the students shortlisted in my college. I decided there to improve my skills and concentrated in Competitive Programming, Web Development, App Development and Blockchain. I actually grew as a developer in this period.

2021 September

This month was quite a special month in my life. I was not a great developer at this period and I just tried to participate in City Command Maps and Location Hackathon conducted by Mitsubishi, NEC And Here APIs. I spent my time sincerly on this hackathon and just learnt more and get well versed in MERN Stack. We won the 2nd runner up place worth a price of 50K INR building our project the MPS Maps Github Repo of MPS Maps

2021 November - 2022 Febuary

My first Work Experience. I was selected as a React Native Developer in a startup company - CodeUnity. I worked in a government project involving TN field policing app. It was a great Experience as it was my first Experience working in a real life project. I recieved a stipend of 10K and it helped me to grow as a developer

2021 December

I attended an interview at RocketLane through the college and earned an internship offer as a Software Engineer role. My intern period begins from May 9 of this year. Am really excited, eager and looking forward to work there
